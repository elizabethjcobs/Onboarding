Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below.

What is Capgemini Invent and what are its tribes and specialisations?
Capgemini Invent is the consulting, digital transformation, and innovation arm of Capgemini Group. It helps businesses innovate and transform through digital solutions and strategies. Capgemini Invent is organised into various tribes, each focusing on specific areas of expertise:
  • Digital Transformation: Focuses on helping clients adapt to digital technologies.
  • Customer Engagement: Specialises in enhancing customer experience through digital channels.
  • Data & Analytics: Provides insights and analytics solutions to drive business decisions.
  • Technology and Engineering: Offers support in implementing advanced technology solutions.
  • Sustainability: Works on integrating sustainable practices into business strategies.
How can I access the resources?
Resources can be accessed through the dedicated resources page, where you can find links to various tools and platforms.
I cannot log into one of the portals, what do I do?
Do not fret, in my first week I was not able to access a few of the portals (i.e. Concur, MyRewards). IT will roll out access to these sites over time. It took over a week for me to gain access to Concur, in personal experience.
I don't know my password for a portal, how do I log in?
A lot of the portals are SSO-based, so if you are logged into your Talent Capgemini account, you will be able to access them (i.e. Replicon, Workzone). However, some of the other portals require you to enter your work email address before requesting a new password (i.e. Expedia, MyRewards).
Do I get a work phone?
As of August 2025, Capgemini has transitioned to a BYOP scheme. They no longer provide work phone devices, though you may be able to request one depending on your assigned project.
What is mandatory training & how do I access it?
Mandatory training includes essential online courses that all employees must complete for compliance and skill development. If you have recently joined Capgemini (prior to October 2025), you will not be able to access these courses as the platform they are hosted on is changing. You will be able to complete these courses from October 2025 onwards.
I'm overwhelmed, what do I do first?
1. Set up your work laptop: Windows or Mac.
2. Check your employee details & submit your P45 to the Payroll Team (in your Outlook email).
3. Log the portals you have access to (i.e. Talent, Workzone, GTD, Replicon) & bookmark them.
4. Sign up for the Buddy Program. This is a great way to meet people within or outside your tribe!
5. Familiarise yourself with Teams & Outlook. This will be your primary source of contact with colleagues.
